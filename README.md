# Atest_pr2
A test repository for a 3d unity fps game with a sort of poppyplatime inspiration but more of a daycare theme( work in progres).
and yes I have modeled the prop and textured her all by myself, where the player uses the doll as a guide,which is voice acted by me.
Game Title: Project Marionette
controls: up, down, left, right and alt keys, and space for jumping.
enemy types: enemy 1: default robot.
enemy 2 : sun/moon from FNAF 
enemy 3: doll
weapons: Sniper, Machine gun and pistol.
known bugs: buttons not properly working and score system.
assets: https://sketchfab.com/feed ( would rather not include all assets with links as they are a lot. lots of assets. Almost evrything is from sketchfab except some posters, that are from pinterest.
Doll: Modeled from scratch, uv unwrapped from scratch and textured from scratch by me using procreate. 
What I am most proud of: I love how my UI came out, and the overall view of it. I am mostly proud of everything that I've made, but I am also proud of my doll prop as that is the key to my storytelling aspect. Unfortunately, some of the things are going to be worked on for improvements. I also wanted to add some voice acting to explain  the storyline
